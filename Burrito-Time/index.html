BURRITO TIME

Now, what are the essential ingredients to a good burrito?

POST 1

First things first. Get yourself a good 'ol tortilla. The most common types are corn and flower.

POST 2

And don't you ever forget the main protein filling! Pork, egg, fish, beef and more...it's all good!

POST 3

The toppings are everything. Get some lettuce, a little pico de gallo, maybe a bag of cheese, tomato...the options are endless!

POST 4

Finally of course, who could forget to have a delectable taco sauce? Maybe it's just red or green chile, maybe it's a sweet or spicy salsa.
